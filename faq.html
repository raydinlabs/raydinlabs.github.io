Frequently Asked Questions

What is the primary focus of Raydin Labs?

Our core R&D focuses heavily on pushing boundaries in aerospace engineering, practical robotics, and custom hardware design (including high-speed PCBs).

Are your design files open source?

Select components and code logic are made available via our GitHub repository. Proprietary research papers require permission to distribute.

Where do you source your hardware?

We work with international suppliers and trusted regional merchants like Evelta Electronics and FlyRobo to ensure component reliability.

I have a specific question about a build, where can I ask it?

Please navigate to the Communities page to ask direct technical questions via our discussion board.
