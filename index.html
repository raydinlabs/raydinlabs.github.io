> SYSTEM_OVERVIEW

Raydin Labs is a premier research and development laboratory dedicated to pushing the boundaries of aerospace integration, robotics traversal, and high-speed hardware engineering.

Our mandate is to design, iterate, and manufacture systems that bridge the gap between experimental theory and physical deployment. From custom printed circuit boards tailored for electromagnetic interference control to fully realized structural rover mechanics, we engineer solutions from the silicon up.
